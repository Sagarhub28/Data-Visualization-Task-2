# Task 2 - Data Visualization and Storytelling

## Objective
Create an interactive dashboard using Power BI to analyze Superstore sales data and present meaningful business insights.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Sample Superstore Dataset

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards
  - Total Sales
  - Total Profit
  - Total Orders
  - Average Discount

- Sales by Category
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Profit by Sub-Category
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Segment
- Top 10 Customers

## Filters
- Region
- Category
- Segment
- Order Date

## Key Business Insights
- Technology generated the highest sales.
- The Consumer segment contributed the largest share of sales.
- The West region recorded the highest sales.
- Copiers and Phones were among the most profitable sub-categories.
- Sales varied throughout the year, with noticeable peaks during certain months.
